Advanced ElectroMagnetic Antenna Research Group Lab

We are a group of young researchers working in the broad areas of advanced electromagnetics. The group is spearheaded by Dr Sukomal Dey, Assistant Professor, Indian Institute of Technology, Palakkad. The research carried out in cutting edge technologies are more focused towards Polarization converters, Absorbers, Frequency Selective Surfaces, Reflective Intelligent Surface, Microwave Imaging for security and biomedical applications, Wireless Power transfer in Biological Tissues and so on.

Currently (2022 Jun), the group is a combination of five PhD Students and four B. Tech students working in the broad area of RF and Microwaves. The team has tremendous progress over the years by contributing to the field of Electromagnetics and Radio frequency devices. The group publishes frequently in top conferences and journals over the years. The team has already achieved numerous milestones in the recent years in the broad area of microwaves.

We are equipped with facilities such as RF anechoic chamber, a four-port Vector Network Analyser, Signal General, Spectrum Analyzer and an MMIC fabrication lab that includes wet chemical etching & automated PCB milling and drilling system.
